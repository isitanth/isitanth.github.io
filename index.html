Hi, I’m Anthony a 25-year-old Solution Architect currently working at Orange Business, where I design and integrate modern digital infrastructures. At Orange, I focus on blending technical depth with business perspective, designing solutions that balance performance, reliability, and real customer needs focused on cloud and more private/on-premises compute infrastructures. I enjoy turning complex architectures into clear, purposeful systems, where engineering precision help customer challenges. That’s where lasting value is created, and where the work of many smart people behind the scenes brings the architecture you’ve designed fully to life. Previously, I worked at Airbus as a Unix/Linux system administrator, where I improved observability in sensitive, large-scale infrastructures and gained hands-on exposure to the diverse technologies and challenges of high-reliability environments — discovering the realities of the job and the operational demands of restricted, military-grade contexts. Beyond work, I explore low-level computing on x86_64 and ARM — diving into memory stacks, registers, and a bit of interactive disassembly with tools like GDB, ObjDump, and IDA. I’m fascinated by how complex a simple Mach-O file can be, and how much performance and precision are packed into the devices we use every day. I also experiment with system tools and automation workflows, often integrating AI to explore how it can simplify and accelerate work across different fields. These tools are incredible time-savers, but I stay mindful not to lose the thread — keeping human understanding at the center. I share some of these experiments and projects on GitHub. I speak French, English, and Russian fluently, and I’m learning Polish. Traveling, meeting people, and discovering how different cultures approach technology and life keeps my curiosity alive — helping me see the world, including places we don’t always think of first, from new perspectives. Always happy to connect and stay curious :)
